THE KICK Enterprise Club

A political home for business leaders aligned with Democratic outcomes — and ready to invest in the infrastructure that makes them possible.

Support THE KICK

What We're Building

THE KICK is a civic platform for aligned leadership. Our members contribute financially and strategically to:

  • Elect Democratic candidates who reflect shared priorities
  • Shape policy direction at the local and state level
  • Build long-term civic infrastructure that outlasts any single campaign
  • Create space for disaffected conservatives who are politically homeless but policy-aligned

Who It's For

Our membership includes founders, executives, investors, and business leaders. What they share is an understanding: that political outcomes require more than values — they require structure, strategy, and sustained investment.

Why We Exist

Political outcomes are too often shaped by theater, not strategy — and by money without alignment. While business leaders have long influenced policy from the sidelines, we've lacked a civic structure to move capital, relationships, and ideas with intentionality.

THE KICK exists to change that. We're building lasting infrastructure for aligned political engagement, built on capital, clarity, and long-view outcomes.

What We Believe

  • That civic investment is serious work, and it deserves systems that match that seriousness.
  • That Democratic outcomes require more than values, they require infrastructure.
  • You don't have to wear a label to do the work. But you do have to show up for it.
  • That we don't need to agree on everything, we just need to move in the same direction.
  • That influence is not a dirty word when it's used to build.

Our Origin

THE KICK was created by a group of civic and business leaders who saw a clear gap. There was no consistent way for serious contributors to influence local Democratic outcomes with focus, strategy, and accountability. Campaigns came and went. Meetings happened. Money moved — but nothing durable was taking shape.

So they built something new: a platform to organize capital, coordinate engagement, and build the political infrastructure they knew would be needed for the long term.

Engagement

Monthly Leadership Lunches

Held monthly, this is THE KICK's central forum for trusted engagement. These gatherings are designed for members to hear directly from elected officials, candidates, policy leaders, and issue experts — without the noise of public forums or the fluff of staged panels.

Each session is grounded in real-time context: what's moving, what matters, and where aligned capital and strategy can have impact. But the most valuable outcome is the one we've designed intentionally: trusted relationships among business leaders who are serious about outcomes, not optics.

A Space for Principled Alignment
Not everyone feels at home in today's political landscape, and that's exactly why this space exists. THE KICK was built for people who think long-term, act with intention, and believe in contributing to outcomes that reflect shared priorities.

We don't define people by where they've been, we organize around strategic alignment: with leaders who understand that lasting change requires more than belief. It requires structure, capital, and people willing to build beyond old affiliations.

Endorsements

THE KICK exists to connect serious resources to serious outcomes — and that includes backing candidates who reflect shared priorities and governing competence.

Our endorsement process is designed to be rigorous, selective, and grounded in long-term thinking. We're building a structure that reflects the values and expectations of our membership: clarity, accountability, and impact.

Coming 2026

We will announce our first cohort of endorsed candidates ahead of the 2026 general election. More information will be shared as the process is finalized — including how members can participate in candidate evaluation and platform alignment.
